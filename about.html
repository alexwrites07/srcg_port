About

Dr. Partha Pratim Mondal


Assistant Professor

IIT (ISM) DHANBAD

Dr. Partha Pratim Mandal is an Assistant Professor at IIT(ISM) Dhanbad, where he teaches basic geophysics and petrophysics and supervises energy transition projects. He received PhD from WASM, Curtin University, Australia. He is also a visiting scientist fellow at CSIRO and a technical advisor at Qeye. From 2011 onwards, he was actively involved in industrial projects, and from 2018 onwards, leading govt/industry-funded research and consultancy projects to improve reservoir characterization and developed comprehensive geomechanical modeling workflow in the energy/resource sectors. He serves as Vice-President of ASEG, WA chapter and technical committee member of IGS.

He received the ASEG Team Member Award from the Australian Society of Applied Geophysics (ASEG) WA for his contribution to society. Awarded HDR RTP fund by the Government of Australia in 2018 for conducting unconventional shale characterization research work at Curtin University. He has been a recipient of AAPG Grants-in-Aid and AIG student bursary.
